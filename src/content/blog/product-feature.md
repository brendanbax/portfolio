## High Level Topic

How to distinguish between a product and a feature - deep dive into productization, understanding value and marketability.

Product solves a macro problem. Feature solves specific pain points. Both need to be quantified and measured against a control, validated for usefulness, and collected to determine global impact to customer.

Consider parallels like cars…

Problem: I need a way to get from A to B. The product is transportation. The features determine how to solve the smaller problems.
