## High Level Topic

Thinking about product design using a pillar metaphor… The solution is comprised of key pillars. Too many, product does nothing well. Too few, the product is narrowly focused (niche market).

As you think about the entire solution, highlight specific touchpoints and capabilities that drive value and solve problems. The collection of all these is the total product - product vs. feature (link to another post).

This is essentially a summary of my product roadmapping exercise… this post should give a summary of the mental model and some practical exercises / workshop ideas to help define product pillars under the top tier solution.

Outline capabilities, cluster under common pain points, align under common vertical.
