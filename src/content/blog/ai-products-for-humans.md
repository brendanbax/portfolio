## High Level Topic

How do you stay rooted in HCD as digital products leverage more of AI?

Overcoming objections like:

fear for my job

mistrust in the tool

misunderstanding of value

As a designer building tools like this, how do you frame your work in a way that keeps the humans in the loop, and what can you do to make them feel like AI is a teammate not threat?

Pull from Planning experience… it’s less about automating plans and more about doing the hard math + making the users more efficient. The users’s objections are often your best KPIs to push towards.

Specifically with automation, your goal isn’t to save company on labor but to enable higher outputs in other areas that aren’t possible without scaling in those directions. AI and Automation can push users to do less mundane work, more engagement work. There’s obviously resistance to do that… an excellent planner may not be an excellent sales person.
